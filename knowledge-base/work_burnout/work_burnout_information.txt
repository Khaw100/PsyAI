Work Burnout Information:

Work burnout is a state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged work-related stress. 
It reduces productivity, motivation, and overall well-being.

Common symptoms:
- Chronic fatigue
- Decreased job satisfaction
- Irritability or mood changes
- Difficulty concentrating
- Feeling detached from work responsibilities

Prevention and recovery include setting boundaries, practicing self-care, seeking support, and reevaluating work balance.
