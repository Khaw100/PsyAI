Relationship Issues Information:

Relationship issues occur when conflicts, misunderstandings, or unmet needs disrupt healthy interactions. 
They can affect romantic partners, family, friends, or colleagues.

Common challenges:
- Communication problems
- Lack of trust or jealousy
- Unresolved conflicts
- Emotional distance or neglect
- Balancing personal needs with relationship demands

Support through counseling, open communication, and conflict resolution can help restore balance and connection.
